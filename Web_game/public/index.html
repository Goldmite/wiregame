How To Play

Click node. Connect wires. Get highscore!

You get points for:

  • Matching colored wires. You get bonus points for matching many same-colored wires in a row (across multiple columns).
  • Reaching the end (Get as many nodes in the last column as you can!).

After each click you are given:

  • A random number [from 1 to column size]. This number represents how many wires will be connected from the node you click. You will not be given the same number for a column.
  • A random color. This is the color of the wire.

Rules:

  1. Going out of bounds (above or below the edge) will end your game.
  2. Even numbers will always prefer to connect wires away from the centerline (your selected starting node's horizontal line).
  3. You must finish a column before you are allowed to the next one.

Congratulations!
